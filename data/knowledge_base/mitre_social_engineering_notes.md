# MITRE-Aligned Notes

Social engineering commonly supports initial access and credential access behaviors.

- Phishing emails are commonly used to capture credentials or deliver malicious links.
- Spear phishing messages are tailored to a business context and may reference vendors, payroll, or leadership.
- Credential harvesting campaigns often combine fear, urgency, and fake login pages.
- Follow-on investigation should validate sender identity, headers, URLs, and any linked domains or attachments.
