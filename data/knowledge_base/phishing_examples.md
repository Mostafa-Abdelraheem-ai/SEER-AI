# Phishing and Smishing Examples

Typical phishing indicators include credential requests, urgent deadlines, threatening account closure, suspicious links, and payment instructions.

Examples:

- "Your mailbox will be disabled unless you sign in immediately."
- "This is the CEO, buy gift cards now and keep this confidential."
- "Claim your refund by verifying your account details."
- "Parcel delivery failed. Pay a small customs fee through this link."

Smishing often uses shortened URLs, prize lures, and mobile-oriented account warnings.
